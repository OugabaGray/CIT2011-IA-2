About MangaShop

My story began with a passion for manga and a dream to make it accessible to everyone in Jamaica and beyond.

Info

MangaShop is a student built e-commerce project dedicated to manga, manhwa, and manhua enthusiasts. It provides a seamless experience for readers to explore, collect, and enjoy their favourite stories online. The project was developed as part of the Web Programming (CIT2011) course at the University of Technology, Jamaica.

My Mission

To connect manga fans across the Caribbean with affordable, authentic, and exciting collections digitally and physically.

My Vision

To become the Worlds’s leading online manga hub, inspiring imagination through art and storytelling.
